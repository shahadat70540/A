biography

Md Shahadat Hossain, the eldest son of a middle-class family, was very interested in information and technology despite not having higher education, so he learned the web developer programming language through blogs, articles and YouTube tutorials. He used to do some web design work with this education. A web design and development course was started by the government of Bangladesh, its name was LEDP. The trainer of that course was KH Muhib sir, through whose tireless efforts I am a web developer today. Working relentlessly for the past 5 years from 2019 to 2024 by the grace of Allah, Alhamdulillah satisfying the clients through work and at the same time helping someone from his family.

Thanks LEDP.
